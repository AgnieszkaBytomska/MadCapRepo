Measuring instruments

Directive 2004/22/EC

This Directive is especially for getting certificates of the authorities of each Member State and is relevant for automatic weighing machinery (cargo scanner, price labeling machinery etc.). Member States may require, if it needed for correct use of the instrument, the information referred to in Annex I or in the relevant instrument specific annexes to be provided in the official language(s) of the Member State in which the instrument is placed on the market. The conformity of a measuring instrument with the essential requirements shall be assessed in accordance with Article 9.
